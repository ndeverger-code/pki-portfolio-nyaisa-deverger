Step 4 — Check Revocation and Trust
-------------------------------------
Q: Is an OCSP URL present in the certificate?
A: Yes — OCSP - URI:http://ocsp.comodoca.com

Q: For an expired certificate, is revocation checking relevant to the remediation? Why or why not?
A: No — revocation checking is not relevant to the remediation. An expired certificate is already rejected by clients before revocation status is ever checked. Expiration is evaluated first in the TLS handshake; a certificate that has passed its Not After date will fail validation regardless of its revocation status. The fix is to renew or replace the certificate, not to revoke it.
